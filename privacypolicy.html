Privacy Policy

  • Privacy Policy

Skylink Infosolutions is a software consultancy that provides customized solutions for various clients across different domains. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal data when you visit our website, contact us, or use our services.


What personal data do we collect ?

We may collect the following types of personal data from you:

  • - Contact information: such as your name, email address, phone number, and postal address.
  • - Technical information: such as your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and other information about your use of our website and services.
  • - Usage information: such as the pages you visit, the links you click, the time and duration of your visits, and other information about your interactions with our website and services.
  • - Feedback information: such as your comments, ratings, reviews, testimonials, and suggestions about our website and services.
  • - Project information: such as your requirements, specifications, preferences, budget, timeline, and other information related to the projects you hire us for or inquire about.

How do we use your personal data ?

We may use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • - To provide you with our website and services: such as responding to your inquiries, delivering the projects you hire us for, providing technical support, and sending you updates and notifications.
  • - To improve our website and services: such as analyzing your usage patterns, conducting surveys and research, testing new features and functionalities, and developing new products and services.
  • - To market our website and services: such as sending you newsletters, promotions, offers, and advertisements that may be of interest to you based on your preferences and profile.
  • - To comply with legal obligations: such as responding to lawful requests from authorities, enforcing our terms and conditions, protecting our rights and interests, and resolving disputes.

How do we share your personal data ?

We may share your personal data with the following parties:

  • - Our service providers: such as web hosting providers, cloud computing providers, analytics providers, email marketing providers, payment processors, and other third-party vendors that help us operate our website and services.
  • - Our clients: such as the organizations or individuals that hire us for software consultancy projects. We may share your project information with them to facilitate the delivery of the projects.
  • - Our partners: such as other software consultancies or developers that we collaborate with on certain projects. We may share your project information with them to ensure the quality and efficiency of the projects.
  • - Our affiliates: such as our subsidiaries or parent companies that are part of our corporate group. We may share your personal data with them for internal management purposes or to offer you related products and services.
  • - Other parties: such as law enforcement agencies, regulators, courts, or other parties when required by law or when necessary to protect our rights and interests.

How do we protect your personal data ?

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction. These measures include:

  • - Using encryption and secure protocols to transmit and store your personal data.
  • - Implementing access controls and authentication mechanisms to limit who can access your personal data.
  • - Conducting regular backups and audits to ensure the integrity and availability of your personal data.
  • - Training our staff and contractors on data protection best practices and policies.

How long do we keep your personal data ?

We keep your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it or as required by law. The retention period may vary depending on the type of personal data and the nature of our relationship with you. Generally, we will delete or anonymize your personal data when:

  • - You withdraw your consent or object to our processing of your personal data (unless we have a legitimate interest or legal obligation to keep it).
  • - You terminate your account or stop using our website or services (unless we need to keep it for legal reasons or ongoing projects).
  • - We complete the project you hired us for or inquired about (unless we need to keep it for legal reasons or future reference).

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

We may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your use of our website. This information may include your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages you visit. We may use this information to personalize your experience on our website and show you content and advertising that is relevant to your interests. You may choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but please note that some features of our website may not function properly if you do so.


Data Security and Retention:

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, and destruction. We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services to you and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.


What are your rights regarding your personal data ?

You have certain rights with respect to your personal information, including the right to access, rectify, and erase your data, as well as the right to object to or restrict the processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at [insert contact email address]. Please note that we may require you to provide additional information to verify your identity before we can process your request.

You have certain rights regarding your personal data under applicable laws. These rights may include:

  • - The right to access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • - The right to rectification: You have the right to request us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.
  • - The right to erasure: You have the right to request us to delete any personal data we hold about you (subject to certain exceptions).
  • - The right to restriction: You have the right to request us to limit how we use or share your personal data (subject to certain conditions).

Changes to this Privacy Policy:
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of the policy.
You have the right to access, correct, update or delete your Personal Information that we hold, including your profile and preference information. You also have the right to object to the processing of your Personal Information, ask us to restrict processing of your Personal Information or request portability of your Personal Information. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.
We take appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure or destruction of data. We restrict access to your personally identifying information to employees who need to know that information in order to operate, develop or improve our services.
We may use your Personal Information to contact you with newsletters, marketing or promotional materials and other information that may be of interest to you.
